Rooted in a user-centered design approach, the Interactive Experience Design (IXD) Lab @FIU seeks to understand how XR and AI can seamlessly extend our thoughts, behaviors, and activities by augmenting today's information interaction experiences. As an interdisciplinary team, we tackle challenging research problems in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), eXtended Reality (XR), Creativity Support Tools (CST) and Accessibility (A11y). We build and evaluate deployable solutions and test their utility in the real world.
