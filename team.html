Faculty

Chen Chen, Ph.D.
Chen Chen is an Assistant Professor in the Knight Foundation School of Computing and Information Sciences. His research interests are in the areas of Human-Computer Interaction, eXtended Reality, and Creativity Support Tools. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of California San Diego, M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University, and B.Eng. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the University of Nottingham, United Kingdom.
